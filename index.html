Our Mission

Thinkstitue’s mission is to offer academic support for flourishing students. Our service provides a focused environment for teachers and students to interact through one-on-one collaboration. Our tutors strive to promote student success and recognize that every student brings unique abilities and learning strategies to our community. At Thinkstitute, we aspire to empower the youth to become successful lifelong learners.

Our Goal

All profits created by our service is directed towards improving the literacy rates of students in third world countries. Thinkstitute supports charities such as the “Asante Africa Foundation” and the “Global Partnership for Education” charity.

Our Intention

With your help, every child can receive proper education in order to succeed in the future.

Reasons for Choosing Us

We Ensure Success

Thinkstitue is an online-based non-profit tutoring platform designed for students in Kindergarten through 12th grade. Tutors work one-on-one with students to improve their academic skills in reading, math, and science. Our tutors strive to promote student success and recognize that every student brings unique abilities and learning strategies to our community. At Thinkstitute, we aspire to empower the youth to become successful lifelong learners.

  • Expert guidance from top high school students.
  • All profits will be donated.
  • Tutoring will happen online only!

Thinkstitute offers individualized self-paced courses in 3 subjects: Math, English, and Science. Material is provided for each course. Students are also welcome to bring in their own material. Classes are scheduled once a week at flexible timings. The duration of classes depends on the student and material difficulty but usually lasts 1 hour.

Charities

Ashante Africa Foundation

Asante Africa Foundation is a global team committed to empowering East Africa’s youth. Their mission is to educate the next generation of change agents, whose dreams and actions transform the future for Africa and the world. Asante develops Africa’s youth through leadership. entrepreneurship, and job readiness. Learn more: https://asanteafrica.org/about-us

Charities

Global Partnership for Education

Global Partnership for Education is exclusively dedicated to giving more children in the poorest countries the education they need to unlock their full potential. GPE’s mission is to mobilize global and national efforts to contribute to the achievement of equitable, quality education and learning for all, through inclusive partnership, a focus on effective and efficient education systems. Learn more: https://www.globalpartnership.org

Charities

Save The Children

For 100 years, Save The Children Foundation has been giving children in the U.S. and around the world a healthy start in life, the opportunity to learn and protection from harm. When crisis strikes, they are always among the first to respond and the last to leave. They do whatever it takes to save children, transforming their lives and the future they share. Learn more: https://www.savethechildren.org/
